NZ Tour Guide Services

Services

NZ Tour Guide Services offers personal Driver/Guiding services for individuals or small groups. We cater to all travellers, holiday makers, families and friends wishing to tour together, in New Zealand.

Including assisting various tourism related small groups, guiding, coordinating, accompanying or driving. This can be groups such as Film Crews, Travel writers & photographers & various visiting Delegations.

Our services are on a private hire or charter basis. We can be available for all or part of your New Zealand visit. Note: We are Christchurch based.

Advanced bookings are essential (as we are busy). Our rates are costed on your actual requirements. Because we deal directly with you, our prices are competitive. We aim to provide a same day reply; with quotes, costings and planning taking a little longer, we are flexible on how we achieve this with you.

PLANNING TOURS:

We can help you plan your touring and sightseeing, in any part of New Zealand. With our expertise in traveling times, distances, and road conditions this can make a huge difference. New Zealand may be a small country, but traveling times can take longer than they may appear on a flat map, a mistake a lot of people including travel agents make!
We can offer tour suggestions,for your tour so that it works,within your time frame and assist with optional type activities,such as scenic boat rides, flights, walks & hikes, attraction, entries etc.

Generally our Quotes Include: a Driver/;Guide,a suitable Vehicle, Fuel, plus the Driver/Guides accommodation and meals.(possibly a relocation fee/flight,prior or after the tour, if required).\n Again we are flexible on how we quote.

DRIVER / GUIDING:

NZ Tour Guide Services aim is to show you the highlights of our beautiful country, while Driver/Guiding you safely,informatively & enjoyably through New Zealand.
We will provide you with a suitable size vehicle for your group or you may wish to hire a vehicle directly yourselves.(providing the vehicle complies with NZTL passenger service regulations).

Most of our clients tend to be from 2 to 8 people (family or friends) with larger groups from 10 to 15 people.

NEW ZEALAND TOURING:

Minimum of 12 days, suggest starting or finishing in Christchurch, Queenstown, Wellington or Auckland, other places by arrangement.(you may need to fly between Islands on tours 12 days or less).

NORTH ISLAND TOURING:

Minimum of 5 days,starting or finishing in Auckland, Wellington, Rotorua or Napier.

SOUTH ISLAND TOURING:

Minimum of 4 days, starting or finishing in Christchurch, Queenstown, Dunedin, Nelson or Picton.

LOCAL TOURING & SIGHTSEEING:

From 1 to 3 days starting & Finishing in Christchurch, suggestions-Kaikoura & Hanmer Springs, West Coast & Arthurs Pass; Mt.Cook & Tekapo or Akaroa.
